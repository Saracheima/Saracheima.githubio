And I will introduce you : the 5 Best Things To Do In London

1. Attend the changing of the guard at Buckingham Palace

If I had to mention just one emblematic tourist attraction in London,
It would surely be this one: the relief of the Royal Guard in front of the Queen of England's palace.
 It is an opportunity to see an amazing show and immerse yourself in the world of the English monarchy.

2. Walking around Hyde Park

Hyde Park is the largest green space in the city.
Like Central Park in New York or Mount Royal Park in
Montreal, Canada, it is one of the most beautiful city parks in the world.
A walk in Hyde Park is a must during a stay in London.
It is also the ideal place to have a picnic in summer, or to have lunch on the terrace by the water.

3. Celebrate in the Soho district

We continue this tourist guide of London by the district of Soho.
Soho is one of the smallest districts in London, but it is also the most lively.
Soho is the centre of London's nightlife and home to all the hottest pubs, theatres, restaurants,
cinemas and discos.

4. Piccadilly Circus

Piccadilly Circus is the city's essential crossroads, and the emblem of modern and commercial
London. Indeed, it is impossible not to have in mind the image of this animated square with huge
advertising screens that broadcast ads 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
It's a bit like London Times Square, in a smaller version.

5. Covent Garden

Among the things to see in London, I recommend that you go to Covent Garden.
The London halls were once located in Covent Garden but, having become too small, they moved in
the 1970s. Covent Garden has fortunately kept its pretty glass roof, which is what makes this place so charming,
and under which many shops, workshops, restaurants and even a museum, the London Transport
Museum, are now located.
